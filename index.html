How It Works

From a GitHub comment to live on Pump.fun

1

Open an Issue

Go to the GitPump repo and open the launch issue. Anyone with a GitHub account can participate.

2

Drop a Comment

Post a /launch command with your token name, symbol, description, and logo attached.

3

Bot Does the Rest

The GitPump bot uploads your logo to IPFS and deploys your token directly on Pump.fun.

4

Go Live

Your token is live on Pump.fun and appears on the GitPump feed for everyone to see.
