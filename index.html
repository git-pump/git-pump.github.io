How It Works

From a GitHub comment to live on Pump.fun

1

Open the Launch Issue

Go to the GitPump repo and open Issue #1. Anyone with a GitHub account can participate.

2

Drop a Comment

Post a /launch command with your token details and attach your logo image.

3

Bot Does the Rest

GitPump uploads your logo to IPFS and deploys your token directly on Pump.fun.

4

Go Live

Your token is live on Pump.fun and appears on the GitPump feed for everyone to see.

github.com/git-pump/gitpump — Issue #1 comment
